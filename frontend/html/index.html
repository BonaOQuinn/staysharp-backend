OUR STORY

The Art of the Cut

Stay Sharp San Diego started as a personal reminder — stay focused, stay centered. Founded by Bebos Diez at just 21 years old with no financial backing, only faith and determination, Stay Sharp has grown into one of San Diego's most recognized barbershops over 9 years in the game.

When COVID shut down our Chula Vista location, it only sharpened the craft. Since then, Bebos has cut for Jason Derulo, Jake Paul, Jimmy Butler, Terry Crews, Swizz Beatz and more — with cuts going viral worldwide surpassing 100+ million views and likes.

"Stay Sharp hasn't been nothing but a self reminder. Feels like this is only the beginning." — Bebos Diez
